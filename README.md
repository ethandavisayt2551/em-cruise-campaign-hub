## What em Does

em is a web-based internal campaign builder for cruise e-shot production. It combines UTM generation, export utilities, and campaign packaging so teams can assemble campaign assets without bouncing between separate tools.

The app is intended for workflows where campaign files, asset folders, and summary outputs need to stay in sync. By keeping those elements together, it helps cut down on manual preparation and makes recurring campaign setup easier to repeat.
